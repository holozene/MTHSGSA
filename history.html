Club History


Changed name: In 2018 we changed the name of the club from Gay Straight Alliance,
to Gender Sexuality Alliance because it seemed more inclusive. We screened the movie
Love, Simon to celebrate the name change.

LGBTQ+ History



The rainbow flag is a symbol for the LGBTQ+ community. The original rainbow flag from 1978 had
8 colors. Hot pink represented sex, red represented life, orange represented healing, yellow
represented sunlight, green represented nature, turquoise represented magic/art, indigo represented
serenity, and violet represented spirit.
